# Privacy Policy for Quille

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

Quille is a writing planning app for creating story pages, outlines, timelines, and long-form creative writing projects.

We do not sell user data. We do not use user data for advertising or third-party tracking.

## Data You Create

Quille allows users to create writing content, including pages, notes, outlines, timelines, and related project information. This content is created by the user and is used only to provide the app's writing and planning features.

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled, user-created content may be stored in the user's personal iCloud account through Apple CloudKit. This data is handled by Apple's iCloud services and is used to sync content across the user's devices.

## In-App Purchases

If Quille offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, purchase information is processed by Apple. Quille does not directly collect or store payment card information.
